# Descriptions and Compliments

## People Descriptions

| Cantonese Phrase (Phenetics)   | English Translation         | Notes                      |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| nay hai lang noi               | You are a beautiful girl    | Compliment                 |
| ore hai chung choy             | I am an idiot               | Self-deprecating statement |
| nay hai tin choy               | You are a genius            | Compliment                 |
| ore pang-yau hai lang zai      | My friend is a handsome man | Describing friend          |
| nay ma-ma hai si fu            | Your mum is a master        | Respectful description     |
| ore ba-ba hai choy zu          | My dad is a rich man        | Describing father          |
| nay hai gwai lo                | You are a western man       | Identifying someone        |
| ore tung-si hai chee sin       | My colleague is a teacher   | Describing colleague       |
| nay lou-baan hai chung choy ma | Is your boss an idiot?      | Question about boss        |

## Color Descriptions

| Cantonese Phrase (Phenetics) | English Translation | Notes                         |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| ore chung-ee hong sik        | I like red          | Color preference              |
| nay chung-ee lam sik ma      | Do you like blue?   | Asking about color preference |
| ore oi luk sik               | I want green        | Requesting green color        |
| hai hong sik                 | Yes, red            | Confirming red color          |
