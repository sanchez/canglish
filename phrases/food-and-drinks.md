# Food and Drinks

## Ordering and Preferences

| Cantonese                       | English                    | Notes                                      |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| ore oi sik                      | I want to eat              | Expressing hunger/desire to eat            |
| nay oi sik ma                   | Do you want to eat?        | Asking if someone wants to eat             |
| ore chung-ee sik guy            | I like to eat chicken      | Expressing food preference                 |
| ore oi cha                      | I want tea                 | Ordering tea                               |
| ore oi gaa fair                 | I want coffee              | Ordering coffee                            |
| ore oi sui                      | I want water               | Ordering water                             |
| nay oi cha ding-hai gaa fair ma | Do you want tea or coffee? | Offering drink choices (missing "or" word) |
| ore chung-ee chan zup           | I like orange juice        | Expressing drink preference                |
| nay chung-ee oiw lai ma         | Do you like milk?          | Asking about milk preference               |
| ore oi oiw yuk meen             | I want beef noodles        | Ordering specific dish                     |
| mmm goi yut ping gaw            | Please one apple           | Ordering one apple politely                |
| ore oi yee dahn                 | I want two eggs            | Ordering two eggs                          |
| ore chung-ee tau fu             | I like tofu                | Expressing tofu preference                 |
| ore oi sik yeu                  | I want to eat fish         | Ordering fish                              |
| nay chung-ee jew yuk ma         | Do you like pork?          | Asking about pork preference               |
| ore oi dahn tart                | I want egg tart            | Ordering egg tart                          |
| ore ho chung-ee ta su bao       | I really like BBQ pork bun | Expressing strong preference               |
| ore mou oiw lai                 | I don't have milk          | Stating lack of milk                       |
| mmm goi saam cha                | Please three teas          | Ordering three teas politely               |
| ore chung-ee her-ung chiew      | I like banana              | Expressing fruit preference                |
| nay oi ping gaw ma              | Do you want apple?         | Offering apple                             |
